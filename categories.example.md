# Post categories / themes (TEMPLATE — copy to categories.md to customize)

The drafter spreads posts across these categories so your feed has variety instead
of being all hot takes. Add, remove, or reword them. `python main.py init` copies
this to `categories.md`, which is the file actually used.

Each category has a name and a short description of what that kind of post is.

- **hot-take** — a strong, specific opinion about a tool, language, or practice.
- **build-in-public** — something you shipped, broke, or are working on right now.
- **learning-in-public** — something that confused you and how you figured it out.
- **reaction** — your take on a trending post or news item from the scraped feed.
- **tip** — a small, genuinely useful thing you learned (a flag, a trick, a gotcha).
- **question** — an honest question to the community that invites replies.
- **story** — a short story or lesson from your dev experience.
